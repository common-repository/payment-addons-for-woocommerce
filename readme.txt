The easiest and fastest way to add 30+ payment methods (Also PayPal) to your WooCommerce website powered by Stripe! Payment methods all-in-one form.

== Description ==

WooCommerce Stripe addon is a payment gateway for WooCommerce. With just a single configuration, unlock access to over 30 secure and globally recognized payment methods, including Credit Cards, PayPal, Google Pay, ApplePay, WeChat Pay, Alipay, Afterpay, ACH, Klarna, iDEAL, FPX, Grabpay, OXXO, Multibanco, Bancontact, Bank Transfer, EPS, P24, Giropay, Affirm, and more.

Yes! In Live Mode, an SSL certificate must be installed on your site to use Stripe.

Yes, it does - You can switch them in plugin setting page.
